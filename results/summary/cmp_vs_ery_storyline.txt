CMP vs Erythroblast: Motivation -> Methods -> Results -> Why
=============================================================

Motivation
----------
Hematopoietic differentiation is expected to involve coordinated shifts in transcript abundance and chromatin accessibility.
Comparing CMP (progenitor-like) versus Erythroblast (more differentiated erythroid state) tests whether erythroid programs are activated and whether nearby chromatin changes support that activation.

Methods
-------
RNA-seq:
- ScriptSeq-only samples with two biological replicates per condition.
- Differential expression run with both DESeq2 and limma-voom on the first pair, as required.
- GO enrichment on DE genes and structure checks using PCA, sample correlation, hierarchical clustering, and heatmaps.

ATAC-seq:
- Pairwise differential accessibility from processed bigBed peaks with consensus peak strategy.
- DAR annotation to nearby genes and GO enrichment.
- Stringent post-processing added for reporting focus: padj < 0.01, |log2FC| >= 2, non-distal annotations, and directional GO.

RNA-ATAC Integration:
- Intersect genes that are significant in both RNA methods with ATAC-linked genes.
- Keep direction-concordant genes (up + open in same cell type) as strongest regulatory candidates.

Results
-------
RNA is robust:
- DESeq2: 4799 DE genes; limma-voom: 5057 DE genes.
- Shared DE genes: 4209, with 100.0% direction agreement.
- Functional themes include erythrocyte differentiation/homeostasis and ribosome/rRNA programs.
- Sample-level plots show replicate coherence and clear separation by cell-line group.

ATAC is broad but still informative:
- 93,160 annotated DAR rows in baseline output; 55,523 in stringent subset.
- Directional stringent GO shows erythroid-consistent terms for Ery-open subset.
- CMP-open subset includes broader and less specific terms, which should be interpreted conservatively.

Integration now complete:
- 629 genes are up in Erythroblast and more open in Erythroblast.
- 1043 genes are up in CMP and more open in CMP.
- These represent direction-concordant multi-omic candidates for mechanistic interpretation.

Why this is happening
---------------------
The data support a differentiation model where erythroblast maturation requires transcriptional activation of erythroid and high-biosynthesis programs.
Concordant RNA+ATAC genes suggest chromatin accessibility changes are part of the regulatory mechanism enabling those expression shifts.
At the same time, ATAC in this run uses bigBed score fallback rather than BAM-derived counts, so accessibility significance should be framed as exploratory rather than definitive.

Figure-by-figure interpretation guide (for slides/report text)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- PCA: Separation by cell-line group indicates strong biological differences; tight replicate grouping indicates acceptable technical consistency.
- Correlation heatmap: Within-group replicate correlations exceed between-group correlations, supporting biological signal over noise.
- Volcano plots: Large-magnitude fold changes in both directions indicate coordinated lineage transition rather than isolated markers.
- Top-feature heatmaps: Expression/accessibility patterns show block structure by cell-line group, visually reinforcing pairwise differentiation.
